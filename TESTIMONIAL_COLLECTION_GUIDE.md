# Testimonial Collection Guide - YPSMA

## Purpose
Collect authentic donor testimonials for social media content (Template 3: Donor Testimonial Wednesdays) and website trust signals.

**Target**: 10 high-quality video testimonials in 30 days
**Method**: WhatsApp outreach to existing donors (237 active donors)
**Timeline**: Start 10 July 2026, complete by 8 August 2026

---

## DONOR SEGMENTATION (Prioritize Outreach)

### Tier 1: High-Value Donors (Contact First)
**Criteria**: 
- Donating ≥6 months continuously
- Package: Adopsi Santri (Rp 250k/month) or Wakaf Produktif
- High engagement (replies to monthly reports)

**Why Prioritize**: Strongest social proof, relatable success stories, likely to say yes

**Estimated Pool**: 40 donors (17% of 237)

---

### Tier 2: Consistent Mid-Tier (Contact Second)
**Criteria**:
- Donating ≥3 months continuously
- Package: Sahabat Pendidikan (Rp 50k/month)
- Moderate engagement

**Why**: Represent majority segment, show accessibility ("gaji UMR bisa donasi")

**Estimated Pool**: 120 donors (51% of 237)

---

### Tier 3: Recent Donors (Contact Third)
**Criteria**:
- Donating <3 months
- Any package
- Still in honeymoon phase

**Why**: Fresh enthusiasm, but less credible long-term impact

**Estimated Pool**: 77 donors (32% of 237)

---

## OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Step 1: Identify Best Candidates (Day 1-2)
**Action**: Review donor database for:
1. Longest donation streak (sort by tenure)
2. Highest total contribution (sort by amount)
3. Most engaged (sort by WhatsApp reply frequency)
4. Geographic diversity (represent different cities)
5. Demographic mix (age, profession, gender balance)

**Selection Criteria**:
- ✅ Donated ≥6 months
- ✅ Never complained/refunded
- ✅ Replies to monthly reports
- ✅ Relatable story (not ultra-rich)
- ✅ Good WhatsApp communication (no drama)

**Output**: List of 30 priority donors to contact

### Step 3: Execute Outreach (Day 3-10)
**Daily Quota**: Contact 5 donors/day (manageable for admin)
**Schedule**: 
- Morning batch (08:00-10:00): 2 messages
- Afternoon batch (13:00-15:00): 2 messages  
- Evening batch (19:00-21:00): 1 message

**Tracking Sheet** (Google Sheets or Excel):

| Donor Name | Package | Tenure | Contact Date | Response? | Format | Usable? | Notes |
|------------|---------|--------|--------------|-----------|--------|---------|-------|
| Siti Aminah | Adopsi | 12 mo | 10 Jul | Yes | Video | ✅ | Great story! |
| Ahmad Zaki | Sahabat | 6 mo | 10 Jul | No | - | - | Follow up 12 Jul |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

**Follow-Up Protocol**:
- No response after 2 days → Send gentle reminder
- No response after 5 days → Mark as "not interested", move to next
- Response but no content → Send example testimonial for inspiration

---

### Step 4: Review & Select (Day 11-14)
**Quality Criteria**:
✅ Authentic (not scripted, genuine emotion)
✅ Relatable (middle-class, not ultra-rich)
✅ Clear audio/video (no background noise)
✅ Addresses objections ("takut ga sampe", "takut ga transparan")
✅ Shows tangible impact ("rezeki lancar", "barokah")

**Select Top 10**:
- 5 video testimonials (highest impact)
- 3 voice note testimonials (convert to audiogram)
- 2 text testimonials (with photo)

**Rejection Reasons** (if content unusable):
- ❌ Audio unclear
- ❌ Too promotional/scripted
- ❌ Doesn't address objections
- ❌ Too short (<30s) or too long (>3 min)
- ❌ Contains sensitive personal info

---

### Step 5: Edit & Publish (Day 15-30)
**Editing Workflow** (per testimonial):

1. **Trim** (keep best 60-90 seconds)
   - Tools: CapCut (mobile) or DaVinci Resolve (desktop)
   - Cut filler words ("ehm", "jadi gitu", pauses >3s)

2. **Add Subtitles** (CRITICAL for reach)
   - Auto-generate via CapCut or Rev.com
   - Proofread for accuracy (especially names, amounts)
   - Style: Yellow text, black outline, bottom 1/3 of screen

3. **Add B-Roll** (if video quality poor)
   - Layer: Photos of santri, classroom, activities
   - Transparency: 30-50% (keep donor visible)
   - Timing: Sync with key phrases

4. **Add Lower Third** (donor identity)
   - Text: "Ibu Siti, Donatur 1 Tahun, Surabaya"
   - Position: Bottom left corner
   - Duration: First 5 seconds + last 3 seconds

5. **Add Outro CTA** (last 5 seconds)
   - Text: "Jadi Donatur Berkah Seperti Bu Siti?"
   - Button animation: "WhatsApp: 082234551160"
   - Background: Semi-transparent overlay

6. **Export Settings**:
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical)
   - Frame rate: 30fps
   - Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
   - Bitrate: 10-15 Mbps

**Publishing Schedule**:
- Post 1 testimonial/week (every Wednesday)
- Rotate between video/voice/text formats
- Cross-post to TikTok + Instagram
- Pin best testimonial on profile

**Store signed forms** (digital copy in Google Drive, organized by donor name)

---

### Privacy Options (Offer to All Donors)
1. **Full Disclosure**: Real name + face + location
2. **Partial Anonymity**: Real name + blur face + generic location ("Surabaya")
3. **Full Anonymity**: Fake name + blur face + no location
4. **Voice Only**: No face, just voice note with text overlay
5. **Text Only**: Quotes with stock photo (no personal image)

**Default**: Option 2 (partial anonymity) unless donor explicitly chooses otherwise

---

### Content Moderation (Before Publishing)
Check for:
- ❌ No mention of competitor organizations
- ❌ No political statements
- ❌ No religious extremism
- ❌ No personal grievances
- ❌ No false claims ("rezeki 10x lipat", etc.)
- ✅ Factual, balanced, authentic

**Approval Process**:
1. Admin reviews raw content
2. Edited version sent to donor for approval
3. Final version published after donor confirmation

## COMMON OBJECTIONS & HOW TO HANDLE

### Objection 1: "Saya ga pede di depan kamera"
**Response**:
"Ga masalah Pak/Bu! Bisa pakai voice note aja kok. Atau kalau mau video, wajah di-blur juga boleh. Yang penting suaranya jelas biar bisa bantu orang lain 😊"

---

### Objection 2: "Takut di-judge orang"
**Response**:
"Tenang Pak/Bu, banyak donatur kita yang minta nama disamarkan. Nanti kami blur wajah + ganti nama jadi 'Ibu A' atau 'Bapak B'. Fokusnya ke cerita, bukan ke orangnya."

---

### Objection 3: "Ga ada waktu"
**Response**:
"Cukup 2 menit aja Pak/Bu 😊 Bisa dijawab lewat voice note sambil nyetir atau sambil istirahat. Ga perlu prepare, spontan aja seperti ngobrol biasa."

---

### Objection 4: "Donasi itu rahasia, ga boleh dipamer"
**Response**:
"Betul Pak/Bu, kalau tujuannya riya' memang ga boleh. Tapi kalau tujuannya mengajak orang lain ikutan donasi (dakwah bil hal), itu justru amal jariyah. Nanti nama Bapak/Ibu bisa disamarkan kok kalau mau privasi."

**Dalil Support** (if needed):
"Rasulullah SAW bersabda: 'Barang siapa menunjukkan kepada kebaikan, maka dia mendapat pahala seperti orang yang melakukannya.' (HR. Muslim)"

---

### Objection 5: "Belum merasakan manfaat signifikan"
**Response**:
"Ga masalah Pak/Bu, justru kami mau dengar pengalaman yang apa adanya. Mungkin baru donasi 3 bulan tapi udah ngerasain ketenangan hati? Atau punya motivasi baru? Itu juga valuable kok buat calon donatur."

## ROLLBACK PLAN (If Response Rate <40%)

**Pivot Strategy**:

1. **Change Incentive**: Offer small gift (Rp 50k-100k voucher)
2. **Shorten Message**: 3 sentences max + 1 question
3. **Call Instead of Text**: Phone calls have 3x response rate
4. **Lower Bar**: Accept 30-second video (instead of 60s)
5. **Use Existing Content**: Repurpose monthly report replies as testimonials

**Emergency Alternative**: If outreach fails completely, use:
- Staff testimonials (admin, teachers)
- Santri testimonials (alumni success stories)
- Partner testimonials (Berkah Karya, local businesses)
